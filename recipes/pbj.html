Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwich

Description

The best and fastest meal comprised of only 3 ingredients.

Satisfy your hunger and spend less on lunch daily!

Easily make it for yourself and all your co-workers. Become the best friend!

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Jam (use your favorite flavor)
  • Peanut butter

Steps

  1. Toast both slices of bread
  2. Begin with the peanut butter. Spread all over 1 side of one of the slices. Wipe all excess peanut butter on the slice and make it clean for the next step.
  3. Continue with the jam. Spread the jam the same way as the peanut butter
  4. Combine both slices with the jam and peanut butter in the center.
  5. Enjoy!!
