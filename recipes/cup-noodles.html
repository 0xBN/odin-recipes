Cup Noodles

Description

The best spicy noodles in town.

Quick and tasty!

Made from a long lineage of samurai dynasty traditions for the modern day man.

Ingredients

  • Shin Noodle Cup
  • 300 ml of hot boiling water

Steps

  1. Open packaging and tear lid off half way.
  2. Empty seasoning pack into the container
  3. Pour 300 ml of hot boiling water into the cup
  4. Close the lid and wait about 3 mins. Note: 2 mins for al dente or 4 mins for extra soft
  5. Enjoy!
